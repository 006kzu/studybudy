# Privacy Policy for Learn Loop

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Learn Loop** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our productivity application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
When you create an account or use the app, we collect:
*   **Account Information:** Your name and email address (via Sign in with Apple or Google).
*   **User Content:** Study goals, session logs, inventory items, and profile customization preferences.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
*   **Usage Data:** We track your interaction with the app logs, study streaks, and session durations to provide analytics.
*   **Device Information:** We collect device identifiers and coarse location data required for functionality and advertising personalization (minimized where possible).

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to operate our app. These partners generally only collect data necessary to perform their specific functions.

### Supabase (Backend & Database)
We use Supabase to host our database and manage user authentication. Your data is stored securely in the cloud.
*   [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

### Google AdMob (Advertising)
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use:
*   Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, Android Advertising ID).
*   Coarse location data.
*   Usage data for ad performance measurement.
*   *Note: We configure our ad settings to be compliant with child-safety regulations.*
*   [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 4. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
*   Sync your progress across devices.
*   Maintain the "Parent Dashboard" and "Leaderboard" features.
*   Display relevant advertisements to keep the basic version of the app free.
*   Improve app performance and fix bugs.

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
We retain your data only as long as your account is active.
*   **Delete Account:** You can request full data deletion at any time by going to **Settings > Delete Account** within the app. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers immediately.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Learn Loop allows for a "Parent Dashboard" experience. We are committed to protecting children's privacy.
*   We do not knowingly sell the personal information of children.
*   Any advertising shown to users known to be under 13 is configured to be **non-personalized** and contextual only, in compliance with COPPA and GDPR-K.
